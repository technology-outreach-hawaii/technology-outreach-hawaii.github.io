Shaping Future Digital Leaders

A 501(c)3 non-profit leveraging the power of our youth to promote technology education, mentorship, collaboration and service across Hawaii.

{% include carousel.html %}

Vision

A Hawaii where everyone has access to technology education and resources.

What We Do

Promote technology education

Technological skills are essential for success in our rapidly advancing world. We utilize neer-peer mentorship to provide up-to-date knowledge and mutual benefits to both mentor and mentee.

Empower youth

Built by students, for students, we empower youth and young adults by providing opportunities for leadership, mentorship, and innovation.

Make resources accessible

All programs are free and supported by the passion and generosity of our volunteers, donors, and sponsors. We strive to provide the technological resources required for learning.
